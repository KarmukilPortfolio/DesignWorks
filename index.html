About me

I'm in the Design Industry with 6+ Years of Experience.

Hi! I'm Karmukil, a passionate product designer worked across 3 domains with a rich background in both B2C and B2B product designs involved in creating user-friendly and engaging end-to-end experiences for websites and mobile products that make people lives easier.
